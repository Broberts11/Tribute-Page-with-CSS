Abraham Lincoln

16th U.S President

The timeframe of Abraham Lincoln

  • Feb 12th, 1809 - Born in Hardin County (now LaRue County), Kentucky
  • 1816 - Moves to Indiana
  • 1832-1834 - Lincoln runs for his first political office, in the Illinois state legislature, and loses. He wins election in his second attempt, in 1834, and then is reelected for 3 more terms
  • 1836 - Lincoln becomes a lawyer
  • Nov 4th, 1842 - Marries Mary Todd Lincoln and has 4 sons
  • 1860 - Lincoln becomes president
  • Nov 19th, 1863 - Lincoln delivers the gettysburg address
  • April 14th-15th, 1865 - John Wilkes Booth shoots President Lincoln, Lincoln dies the next day
